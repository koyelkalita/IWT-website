Importance of a Schedule

A well-structured schedule is key to success in any field. It helps to allocate time effectively, ensuring that important tasks are given the attention they need. Schedules enable you to set clear priorities, avoid procrastination, and maintain balance in your daily activities.

Following a schedule can also reduce stress by breaking large projects into manageable tasks and providing a sense of control over your time. A consistent routine helps build discipline and productivity, fostering both personal and professional growth.

Pomodoro Technique
A technique that involves working in short intervals with regular breaks.
Delegation
Assigning tasks to others to lighten your load and increase efficiency.
